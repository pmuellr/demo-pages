2017-01-25-playbug - early prototype of playbug

Playback of a playbug session; all you can really do is hit the < and > buttons on the right side of the window, to have the session go forward or backward a step. The source code and frame / scope information on the left get updated as you step. You can also use the cursor keys (left and right) to step.

The bits at the top of the page are place-holders / dummy values for things I anticipate making functional.
